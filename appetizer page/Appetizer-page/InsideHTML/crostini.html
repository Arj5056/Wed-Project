Tomato Basil Bruschetta on Toasted Crostini

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes:4 medium ripe tomatoes or 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced.
  • Fresh Basil: ¼ cup, finely chopped.
  • Garlic cloves, minced.
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons, extra virgin preferred.
  • Balsamic Vinegar 1 tablespoon, for tanginess.
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste.
  • Baguette: 1 loaf, sliced into ½-inch thick rounds.
  • Olive Oil: 3 tablespoons, for brushing.
  • Garlic Clove: 1, halved, for rubbing the bread.

Directions

Step 1

Prepare the Bruschetta Topping

  1. In a medium bowl, combine diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.


  2. Toss gently to combine. Let sit for 10–15 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

Step 2

Toast the Crostini

  1. In a medium bowl, combine diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.


  2. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet. Brush each slice with olive oil on one side.


  3. Bake for 8–10 minutes, or broil for 2–3 minutes per side, until golden and crisp.


  4. While still warm, rub the toasted side of each slice with the cut side of a garlic clove.

Step 3

Assemble the Bruschetta

  1. Spoon the tomato basil mixture onto each toasted crostini.


  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and garnish with Parmesan shavings, if desired.
