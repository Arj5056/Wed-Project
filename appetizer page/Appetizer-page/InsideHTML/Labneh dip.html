labneh dip with za'atar

Ingredients

  • 10 oz. plain Greek yogurt (plain coconut yogurt for vegan)
  • dash of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon za'atar, for garnish
  • 1 1/4 cup pomegranate arils, for garnish
  • 5 1/4 cup chopped pistachios, for garnish
  • 4 fresh mint leaves, chopped, for garnish
  • 4 medium firm bananas, sliced
  • 1 package Middle Eastern flatbread

Directions

Step 1

Make homemade labneh for a better experience...
in a small bowl, stir together the yogurt, lemon juice, and salt.

Step 2

Line a fine mesh sieve with cheesecloth and fill with the yogurt mixture.
Fold the cheesecloth back over the yogurt.
Set the sieve with the cheesecloth and yogurt over a bowl.
Refrigerate 12-24 hours, until the labne has reached your desired thickness.

Step 3

Transfer the labneh to a shallow serving dish and drizzle with olive oil
Sprinkle with za'atar, pomegranate, pistachios, and mint.

Step 4

Warm the flatbread and cut into triangles. Serve immediately.
