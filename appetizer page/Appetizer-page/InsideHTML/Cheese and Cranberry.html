Cheese and Cranberry

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe but firm pears (Bosc or Anjou), halved and cored
  • ½ cup crumbled goat cheese
  • ¼ cup dried cranberries, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and freshly cracked black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: Chopped walnuts for garnish

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a baking dish or line it with parchment paper for easy cleanup.

Step 2

Prepare the Pears: Prepare the Pears

Step 3

Arrange the Pears: Place the prepared pear halves cut side up in the baking dish. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the top of the pears to encourage caramelization and bring out their natural sweetness during baking.

Step 4

Add the Filling: Fill each pear half with crumbled goat cheese and chopped dried cranberries, dividing the filling evenly among the pears. The creamy cheese will soften and melt as the pears bake, creating a luscious texture.

Step 5

Bake: Transfer the baking dish to the oven and bake for 15–20 minutes. The pears should be tender when pierce d with a fork but should still hold their shape. The goat cheese will begin to soften and melt slightly, blending p erfectly with the cranberries.

Step 6

Finish and Serve: Once out of the oven, drizzle the pear s generously with honey for a touch of sweetness . Sprinkle fresh thyme leaves over the top, along with a pinch of salt and freshly cracked black pepper to enhance the flavors. If desired, garnish with chopped walnuts for an extra layer of crunch and texture.
