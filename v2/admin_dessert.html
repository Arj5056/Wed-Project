DESSERTS

Banana Pudding with Cream Cheese

The pretty, trifle-style presentation makes this creamy banana pudding with cream cheese extra fabulous. Pudding, sliced bananas and vanilla wafers create each scrumptious layer!

Oreo Mousse Cake

Oreo mousse cake is so simple to put together, but it tastes as decadent as fancy restaurant desserts. Have fun garnishing it as you like, using crushed or whole Oreos, additional whipped cream rosettes or chocolate shavings.

Rainbow Waffle Sandwiches

Proof that Belgian waffles taste even better when covered with sprinkles. Cool down on a hot day with this fun no-bake summer treat that kids will love!

Buckeye Peanut Butter Balls

It really doesn't get much simpler than these classic bites. They require just five ingredients and come together in 20 minutes.

No-Bake Cheesecake with Blueberry Sauce

Sometimes you just can't beat a classic no-bake cheesecake. We'll have extra berry sauce with ours.

Cake Pops

This easy treat be customized with different flavors and decorations. Use it to make chocolate cake pops, holiday cake pops and more.

Individual Key Lime Pies

Ree's adorable Key lime pies come together in three easy layers. Spread homemade lime curd atop buttery graham cracker crust, then add a dollop of fresh whipped cream and garnish with lime zest.

Coconut Rice Pudding

Rice isn't just for savoury dishes why not enjoy it for dessert, too? This rice pudding has a vibrant tropical fruit topping.
