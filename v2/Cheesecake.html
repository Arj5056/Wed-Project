No-Bake Cheesecake with Blueberry Sauce

Ingredients

  • ½ cup / 74 g Blueberry
  • ½ cup / 100 g Sugar
  • 1 tbsp / 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 8 pieces / 8 pieces Digestive biscuits
  • 4 tbsp / 4 tbsp Butter (Melted)
  • 1.5 cup / 340.5 g Cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp / 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp / 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  • 1 cup / 238 g Heavy cream

Directions

Step 1

All the ingredients (cream cheese, sour cream and eggs) should be room temperature before getting started. This helps everything mix together easily.

Step 2

Beat the cream cheese and sugar so there are no lumps the last thing you want is lumps in your cheesecake. Using sour cream in the recipe creates a smoother texture.

Step 3

Add in the eggs at the very end, and stop beating the batter as soon as they're incorporated. When beating the eggs it adds air to your mixture. More air = more chance of your cheesecake cracking.

Step 4

Spoon the cream cheese mixture over the chilled biscuit base Spread evenly using a spatula. Place the cake pan in fridge for 4 to 6 hours Once the cheesecake is set. Spoon some of the blueberry topping on top. Place it back in fridge until set.
